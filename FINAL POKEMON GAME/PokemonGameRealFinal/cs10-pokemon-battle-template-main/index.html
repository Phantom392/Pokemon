POKEMON


Instructions:
Press your game mode then choose your pokemon.
Once in the game your health bar is displayed beside your character in the HP bar and your ultimate energy is stored in the UP bar.
You can only use your ultimate (3rd attack) once the UP bar is full.
You can only heal 3 times per game.
The two first powers do almost equal damage but your secondary power does more damage the lower health you have.
Different Pokemon can deal higher or lower damage to others based on their element.
To reset the game refresh the page.
